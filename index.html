Animal Attack

Welcome to "Animal Attack", the game where you defend our planet against a hoard of angry animals trying to take over! Apparently, they got tired of living in the wild and decided to start a coup. But you're not going to let that happen, are you? As the only line of defense, you must protect Earth from these militant mammals and their army of critters. Armed with a high-tech animal repeller and a healthy sense of humor, you'll face off against an onslaught of beasts with attitude. From a snarling grizzly bear to a scheming group of snakes, these animals are determined to make you their pet. But you're not one to be caged, are you? You'll fight tooth and nail to defend your planet and show these rebellious animals who's boss. So suit up, grab your animal repeller, and get ready to send these pesky creatures back to the zoo where they belong!
